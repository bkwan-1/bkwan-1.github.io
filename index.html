About Vancouver Nonprofit Hub

Connecting communities through the power of giving back

Our Story

Vancouver is home to hundreds of incredible nonprofits working tirelessly to make our city a better place. Yet many of these organizations struggle to find volunteers and supporters, while passionate residents don't know where to start when they want to give back.

Vancouver Nonprofit Hub was created to bridge this gap. We're building a comprehensive directory that makes it easy to discover local nonprofits, learn about their missions, and get involved in causes you care about.

Our platform celebrates the diversity of nonprofit work happening across Vancouver — from environmental conservation to youth empowerment, from arts and culture to housing justice. Every organization listed here is contributing to a stronger, more connected community.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To amplify the impact of Vancouver nonprofits by connecting them with passionate volunteers, donors, and community members who share their vision for positive change.

Community First

We believe in the power of local action. Every nonprofit on our platform is making a real difference in Vancouver, and we're committed to supporting their work.

Accessibility

Finding ways to contribute should be simple. We've built a platform that makes it easy for anyone to discover opportunities that align with their passions and skills.

Celebration

We celebrate the incredible diversity of causes and approaches in Vancouver's nonprofit sector. Every organization brings unique value to our community.
